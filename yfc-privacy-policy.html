01

Who we are

Yaariyan Fitness Club (YFC) is a gym based in Chharra, Aligarh, India. This policy explains how we handle information when you interact with us — whether in person, by phone, or through our social media channels.

02

Information we collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide our services. This may include:

  • Your name and contact number when you enquire or register as a member
  • Messages you send us via WhatsApp or Instagram direct messages
  • Comments or interactions on our Instagram page

We do not collect sensitive health or payment data through digital channels at this time.

03

How we use your information

The information we collect is used solely to:

  • Respond to your queries and membership enquiries
  • Share updates, schedules, and offers with you
  • Provide better customer service

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

04

Third-party platforms

We communicate through the following platforms. These have their own privacy policies that govern how your data is handled on their side:

  • WhatsApp (Meta Platforms) — used for direct communication with members and prospects
  • Instagram (Meta Platforms) — used to share content and respond to messages and comments

We encourage you to review Meta's privacy policy at Meta's Privacy Center.

05

Data retention

We retain your contact information only for as long as it is necessary to serve you. If you wish to have your information removed, simply reach out to us and we will delete it promptly.

06

Your rights

Under applicable Indian law, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw your consent to be contacted

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

07

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be communicated through our WhatsApp or Instagram channels. The effective date at the top of this page will always reflect the latest version.
